HAIR IS MORE THAN JUST APPEARANCE

Hair historically holds large importance in the African-American culture. This appreciation of Black hair dates back to ancient Africa, and current-day Africa, where hair was used as a factor to distinguish different tribes. Black hair is diverse, intricate, and to be treated with care.
